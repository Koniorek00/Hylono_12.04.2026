# Phase 0.1: Meta-Discovery Ingestion (The Search for Topics)

**Context**: Hylono is a "Day 1" Startup.
**Goal**: Find the 5 most valuable topics to brainstorm about.
**Participants**: Full Agent Constellation.

---

## 1. STRATEGY-EXECUTIVE (The CEO)

- **Topic Proposal**: "The 'Killer' Feature Discovery". What is the one feature that makes us 10x better than competitors?
- **Topic Proposal**: "Cash Flow Survival Strategy". How do we generate cash in Month 1 without massive ads?
- **Topic Proposal**: "The 'Company-in-a-Box' Operating Model". How exactly do we package this for licensing?

## 2. COMPLIANCE-GATE (The Lawyer)

- **Topic Proposal**: "The Liability Shield". How do we sell medical devices without being sued for medical malpractice?
- **Topic Proposal**: "Data Privacy as a Product". Can we sell 'Privacy' as a premium feature vs competitors who sell data?
- **Topic Proposal**: "Regulatory Arbitrage". Which devices are easiest to approve/sell first?

## 3. GROWTH-MARKETING (The Marketer)

- **Topic Proposal**: "The 'Zero-CAC' Channel". How do we get customers for $0? (Viral loops, SEO, Content).
- **Topic Proposal**: "Trust Architecture". Why should anyone trust a Day 1 website with their health?
- **Topic Proposal**: "The 'Offer' Construction". How do we structure the rental offer so it's a 'No Brainer'?

## 4. PRODUCT-ARCHITECT (The UX)

- **Topic Proposal**: "The 'Onboarding' Flow". The first 5 minutes of a user's life with Hylono.
- **Topic Proposal**: "Digital Twin Integration". Connecting the physical device to the digital dashboard.
- **Topic Proposal**: "Gamification of Health". How to make HBOT addictive (in a good way).

## 5. OPS-ROUTER (The COO)

- **Topic Proposal**: "Logistics of Heavy Things". Shipping 200lb chambers is a nightmare. Brainstorming last-mile solutions.
- **Topic Proposal**: "The 'Human-in-the-Loop' Protocol". When does a human NEED to intervene? (Support efficiency).

## 6. QA-VERIFIER (The Skeptic)

- **Topic Proposal**: "The 'Scam' Test". Does our website look like a scam? Brainstorming credibility markers.
- **Topic Proposal**: "Disaster Recovery". What happens if a chamber fails while a user is inside?
