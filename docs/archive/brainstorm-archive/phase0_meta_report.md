# Phase 0.4: Convergence (The Top 5 Topics)

**These are the 5 topics we will now Deep-Dive Brainstorm.**

## 1. 🛡️ Trust Architecture

* **Question**: "How do we make a Day 1 website feel like a robust, 10-year-old medical institution?"
- **Why**: Without trust, high-ticket sales ($20k) are impossible.

## 2. 🚀 The Zero-CAC Channel

* **Question**: "How do we acquire our first 100 customers for $0 using viral loops, content, or partnerships?"
- **Why**: Cash preservation is survival.

## 3. 💎 The 'Killer' Feature (Differentiation)

* **Question**: "What is the ONE feature that makes a user choose us over a cheaper competitor?"
- **Why**: We need a wedge to enter the market.

## 4. ⚖️ The Liability Shield

* **Question**: "How do we aggressively sell health benefits without crossing the FDA 'Medical Advice' red line?"
- **Why**: We need to market hard but safely.

## 5. 🚚 Logistics of Heavy Things (Ops)

* **Question**: "How do we make the delivery/setup of a 200lb chamber feel magical, not a burden?"
- **Why**: This is the biggest friction point for users.
