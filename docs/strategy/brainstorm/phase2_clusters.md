# Phase 2: Semantic Clustering (Vectorized Concepts)

**Input**: 35+ Raw Ideas from Phase 1.
**Process**: Filter, Tag, Merge.
**Output**: 4 Mega-Concepts.

---

## Mega-Concept A: "The Scientific Trust Engine" (Truth & Safety)

*Theme: "We verify, so you don't have to."*

- **Core Ideas**:
  - **The Truth Log**: Immutable blockchain-style record of claims vs research papers.
  - **Contraindication Engine**: Interactive body map that filters out unsafe devices (Safety First).
  - **Live Research Feed**: Real-time PubMed integration with AI summaries.
  - **Doctor-In-The-Loop**: On-demand MD review of cart/safety profile.
  - **The No-Placebo Guarantee**: Refund if biomarkers don't move (requires N=1 Kit).

## Mega-Concept B: "Frictionless Operations" (Logistics & Flow)

*Theme: "It just works, like magic."*

- **Core Ideas**:
  - **Visual Supply Chain Map**: Uber-style tracking for high-value devices.
  - **One-Click Restock**: Physical IoT buttons or "Smart Reorder" logic.
  - **RentalReturn AI**: AI computer vision to verify return packing (reduces disputes).
  - **Real-Time Inventory Mesh**: Decentralized inventory across partner clinics.
  - **Self-Healing Forms**: Checkout forms that degrade gracefully to capture leads.

## Mega-Concept C: "High-Velocity Growth" (Viral & Financial)

*Theme: "Aggressive Expansion."*

- **Core Ideas**:
  - **The Comparison Destroyer**: Auto-generated "Us vs Them" tables scraping live competitor data.
  - **Hylono Capital**: Financing for clinics based on future revenue.
  - **Referral Trees**: Visual gamification of user networks and earnings.
  - **Dynamic Pricing 2.0**: Decaying rental prices (Rent-to-Own curve).
  - **Abandoned Cart Video**: personalized AI video outreach.

## Mega-Concept D: "The Bio-Optimization Ecosystem" (Product & sticky)

*Theme: "Not a device, but a lifestyle OS."*

- **Core Ideas**:
  - **The Hylono Bio-Index (HBI)**: Single score (1-100) for user health status.
  - **Morning Protocol Push**: SMS/App triggers synchronizing device use with circadian rhythm.
  - **Hylono API (HAPI)**: Developer platform for third-party apps (Oura/Whoop integration).
  - **100-Year Warranty**: The ultimate confidence signal.
  - **Mechanism 3D Explainer**: WebGL education tools.
