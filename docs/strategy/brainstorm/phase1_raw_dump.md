## 1. STRATEGY-EXECUTIVE-ORCHESTRATOR (The Vision)

- **Idea**: "Hylono Capital". A financing arm for clinics. "Buy now, pay with your patient revenue."
- **Idea**: "The 'No-Placebo' Guarantee". If your biomarkers don't improve in 90 days, full refund. Requires partnership with lab testing.

- **Idea**: "Visual Supply Chain Map". A public-facing map showing exactly where their device is, down to the truck on the highway (Uber-style).
- **Idea**: "One-Click Restock". A physical IoT button we send with consumables (cannulas, masks). User presses it, order placed instantly.
- **Idea**: "The 'Morning Protocol' Push". SMS/WhatsApp trigger at 7 AM: "Time for your 20m HBOT session. Oxygen is ready."

- **Idea**: "Hylono API (HAPI)". A public API for developers to build apps on top of our devices (e.g., "Start HBOT when Oura Ring detects low recovery").
