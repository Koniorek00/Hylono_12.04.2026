# Intent Model

- Primary user intent: Primary intake / contact route.
- Primary business intent: Support the route role without breaking the wider Hylono architecture.
- Primary action: Submit inquiry
- Secondary actions: Callback, Help, FAQ
- Funnel stage: Public journey support
- Trust burden: High
- SEO importance: Relevant
- Conversion importance: Route-dependent
- Accessibility sensitivity: Medium
- Performance sensitivity: Medium
- Confidence level: High
