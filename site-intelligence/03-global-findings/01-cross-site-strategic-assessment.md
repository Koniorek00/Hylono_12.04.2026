# Cross-Site Strategic Assessment

The current site architecture understands the right business story better than the current public content layer does. Hylono is trying to be a premium, trust-led operator with a guided access model, but parts of the site still behave like a gadget catalog, a manifesto microsite, or a placeholder trust layer.

Highest-level strategy problems:
- The access layer exists, but it is not consistently explained.
- The trust layer exists, but it is undermined by a few synthetic-looking routes.
- The commercial architecture is split between a newer premium hydrogen model family and an older generic PDP system.
- Support routes are functionally real, but their public IA is muddy.
