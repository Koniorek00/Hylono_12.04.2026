# Research Method

- Official docs, official repos, official examples, and official release sources were prioritized.
- Community guides, issues, Reddit, and secondary material were used only where they added practical deployment or operational detail.
- Evidence labels:
  - CONFIRMED
  - INFERRED
  - UNVERIFIED
  - MISSING
- 2024–2026 sources were prioritized.
- Older assets are flagged [POTENTIALLY OUTDATED].
- License posture is recorded and flagged for human review where copyleft, commercial, or unclear terms appear.
