## Phase A — App identity
- **What it is:** Open-source-first e-signature platform for signing workflows, document templates, recipients, and audit trails. [https://docs.documenso.com/, 2026-04-12, current]
- **What it solves:** Digital agreements, waivers, contracts, consents, and internal approvals with a self-hosted or controlled deployment model. [https://github.com/documenso/documenso/releases, 2026-04-12, v2.8.0]
- **Best-fit users:** Operations, legal, HR, and customer-facing teams that need signatures without depending entirely on closed SaaS. [https://docs.documenso.com/, 2026-04-12, current]
- **Where it fits in a modern stack:** High fit for waivers, rental agreements, acknowledgements, and premium service documents—provided Hylono carefully defines what is commercial, legal, or health-adjacent. [https://github.com/documenso/documenso, 2026-04-12, v2.8.0]
