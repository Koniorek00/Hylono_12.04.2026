- **What it solves:** Cookie banners, consent categories, script gating, and front-end consent UX. [https://github.com/orestbida/cookieconsent/releases, 2026-04-12, v3.1.0]
- **Best-fit users:** Web teams that need basic consent management without a heavy CMP platform. [https://cookieconsent.orestbida.com/, 2026-04-12, v3.1.0]
