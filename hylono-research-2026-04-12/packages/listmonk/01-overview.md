## Phase A — App identity
- **What it is:** Self-hosted newsletter and mailing-list manager with campaigns, subscribers, templates, and APIs. [https://listmonk.app/docs/, 2026-04-12, v6.1.0]
- **What it solves:** Email newsletters, list management, segmentation, subscription handling, and lightweight marketing messaging. [https://github.com/knadh/listmonk/releases, 2026-04-12, v6.1.0]
- **Best-fit users:** Marketing and content teams that want a simple, self-hosted newsletter system. [https://listmonk.app/docs/, 2026-04-12, v6.1.0]
- **Where it fits in a modern stack:** Good fit for straightforward newsletter and broadcast email. Cleaner and lighter than Mautic when advanced marketing automation is not yet necessary. [https://github.com/knadh/listmonk, 2026-04-12, v6.1.0]
