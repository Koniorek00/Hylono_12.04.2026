## Phase A — App identity
- **What it is:** Open-source document signing and forms platform focused on self-hosted e-sign workflows. [https://www.docuseal.com/docs, 2026-04-12, current]
- **What it solves:** Signing documents, collecting fields/forms, and hosting signature flows on-prem or in controlled infrastructure. [https://github.com/docusealco/docuseal/releases, 2026-04-12, 2.4.3]
- **Best-fit users:** Teams that need a lighter-weight self-hosted signature tool. [https://www.docuseal.com/docs, 2026-04-12, current]
- **Where it fits in a modern stack:** Reasonable fit, but overlaps strongly with Documenso. For Hylono, Documenso looks like the better primary candidate unless DocuSeal’s simpler AGPL stack is preferred. [https://github.com/docusealco/docuseal, 2026-04-12, 2.4.3]
