## Phase A — App identity
- **What it is:** Static site generator for docs, blogs, and knowledge sites using React/Markdown/MDX. [https://docusaurus.io/docs, 2026-04-12, v3.10.0]
- **What it solves:** Public documentation portals, help centers, knowledge sites, and documentation-driven content publishing. [https://github.com/facebook/docusaurus/releases, 2026-04-12, v3.10.0]
- **Best-fit users:** Engineering, docs, and content teams who need a versionable docs site. [https://docusaurus.io/docs, 2026-04-12, v3.10.0]
- **Where it fits in a modern stack:** Useful if Hylono wants a separate docs/help site, but the public website is already Next.js 16 and server-first. Docusaurus is optional, not default. [https://github.com/facebook/docusaurus, 2026-04-12, v3.10.0]
