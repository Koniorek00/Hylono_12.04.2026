## Phase A — App identity
- **What it is:** Open-source virtual classroom and web conferencing platform with breakout rooms, whiteboards, recordings, polls, and LMS integrations. [https://docs.bigbluebutton.org/administration/install, 2026-04-12, v3.0.x]
- **What it solves:** Structured online classes, webinars, and teaching sessions beyond simple meetings. [https://github.com/bigbluebutton/bigbluebutton/releases, 2026-04-12, v3.0.23]
- **Best-fit users:** Education, training, and webinar operators who need classroom-grade features. [https://docs.bigbluebutton.org/administration/install, 2026-04-12, v3.0.x]
- **Where it fits in a modern stack:** Potentially useful for protocol classes or guided sessions, but operationally heavy. Jitsi is likely the simpler default unless classroom-specific features are truly required. [https://github.com/bigbluebutton/bigbluebutton, 2026-04-12, v3.0.23]
