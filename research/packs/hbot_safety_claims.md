# HBOT Safety & Education-Only Claims

## 1. Core Principle
Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy (HBOT) in a wellness context must NEVER claim to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any specific disease. Language must focus on physiologic optimization rather than disease intervention.

## 2. Approved "Wellness" Language (Safe Zone)
- "Optimizes cellular oxygenation"
- "Supports mitochondrial efficiency"
- "Enhances ATP energy production"
- "Aids in reducing oxidative stress"
- "Supports post-workout recovery"
- "Helps flush metabolic waste (lactate)"
- "Promotes natural regeneration cycles"
- "Supports cognitive clarity and focus"
- "Revitalizes energy levels"
- "Promotes deep relaxation"
- "Supports healthy circulation"

## 3. Forbidden Claims (Red Zone)
- "Cures Cancer"
- "Treats Autism / ASD"
- "Heals Lyme Disease"
- "Prevents Alzheimer's"
- "Fixes Long COVID"
- "Guaranteed healing"
- "Reverses aging"
- "Alternative to surgery"

## 4. Mandatory Disclaimers
> "Hylono chambers are designed for wellness and bio-optimization. They are not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Always consult a healthcare professional before starting any new protocol."

## 5. Evidence Summary (Trace Sources)
- **FDA**: Wellness use is general wellness.
- **Reference**: Claim Policy (claim_policy.yml).
