# Red Light Therapy Safety & Claims

## 1. Core Principle
Red Light Therapy (RLT) and Near-Infrared (NIR) therapy must be framed as supportive of mitochondrial energy and natural regeneration. Claims regarding the treatment of clinical skin conditions or vision correction are forbidden.

## 2. Approved "Wellness" Language (Safe Zone)
- "Supports mitochondrial energy production (ATP)"
- "Promotes a healthy, radiant glow and skin resilience"
- "Assists in post-exercise recovery and fatigue management"
- "Helps regulate natural sleep-wake cycles through light exposure"
- "Enhances cellular vitality and resilience"

## 3. Forbidden Claims (Red Zone)
- "Cures skin cancer"
- "Treats eczema"
- "Improves eyesight"
- "Cures chronic inflammation"
- "Permanent fat loss"

## 4. Mandatory Disclaimers
> "Red Light Therapy is designed for wellness and bio-optimization. It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease. Avoid direct eye exposure unless specifically designed for ocular use."

- **User asks**: "Can I use RLT to treat my skin cancer?"
