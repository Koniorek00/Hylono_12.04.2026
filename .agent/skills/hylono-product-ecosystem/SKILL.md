## The Four Modalities

### 🫁 Oxygen — mHBOT (Mild Hyperbaric Oxygen Therapy)
- **Technology**: Pressurized chamber delivering oxygen at 1.3-1.5 ATA
- **Mechanism**: Increased atmospheric pressure dissolves more oxygen in blood plasma
- **Research areas**: Recovery, cognitive function, wound healing, inflammation reduction
- **Use context**: 60-90 min sessions, lying/sitting in portable chamber
- **Target users**: Athletes, professionals, recovering individuals, biohackers
- **Safety notes**: Contraindications include certain lung conditions, pregnancy, untreated pneumothorax

### 💧 Hydrogen — H₂ Therapy
- **Technology**: Hydrogen gas inhalation or hydrogen-rich water
- **Mechanism**: Selective antioxidant, reduces oxidative stress
- **Research areas**: Inflammation, cellular health, metabolic support
- **Use context**: 20-60 min inhalation sessions or hydrogen water consumption
- **Target users**: General wellness, aging support, inflammation management
- **Safety notes**: Extremely safe profile; avoid open flames (flammable gas)

### 💡 Light — RLT/NIR (Red Light Therapy / Near-Infrared)
- **Technology**: LED panels emitting 630-850nm wavelengths
- **Mechanism**: Photobiomodulation — stimulates mitochondria, increases ATP production
- **Research areas**: Skin health, muscle recovery, pain management, circadian rhythm
- **Use context**: 10-20 min sessions, targeted or full-body panels
- **Target users**: Skin health focus, athletes, sleep support
- **Safety notes**: Eye protection required; avoid direct eye exposure

### 📡 Signal — PEMF + VNS
- **PEMF** (Pulsed Electromagnetic Field):
  - Technology: Electromagnetic pulses penetrating tissue
  - Research areas: Bone density, pain management, sleep quality
  - Use context: Mat/pad worn/sat on for 20-60 min
- **VNS** (Vagus Nerve Stimulation):
  - Technology: Non-invasive auricular or transcutaneous stimulation
  - Research areas: Stress, heart rate variability, cognitive function, mood

## Protocol Stacking
Hylono's USP includes stacking modalities for synergistic effects:
- **Recovery Protocol**: mHBOT + RLT (oxygen + cellular repair)
- **Cognitive Protocol**: PEMF + Hydrogen (neuroprotection + focus)
- **Sleep Protocol**: RLT (evening) + PEMF mat
- **Performance Protocol**: mHBOT + RLT + Hydrogen

## Content Principles by Modality
- Lead with EXPERIENCE (what it feels like), not mechanism
- Follow with EVIDENCE (what research suggests), not claims
- End with ACCESS (how to try it), not just purchase
