# SKILL: Hylono Brand Identity
**Used by**: ceo-auditor, design-system-architect, content-product-writer, visual-asset-director, i18n-specialist, docs-specialist

---

## Brand Essence
**Mission**: Democratizing non-invasive regeneration technology — making premium wellness tech accessible to everyone, not just the wealthy or clinical.
**Tagline**: "where mind connects with matter"
**Positioning**: The trust layer + access layer between cheap marketplaces and expensive clinical brands.

## Personality
- **Premium but approachable** — Not luxury-snob, not discount-casual. Think: accessible luxury.
- **Confident but humble** — We know the technology works. We don't overclaim.
- **Scientific but human** — Evidence-based, never cold or clinical.
- **Empowering** — We give access and guidance, not dependency.

## Voice & Tone
| Context | Tone |
|---------|------|
| Product pages | Warm, confident, educational |
| Safety information | Clear, direct, no-nonsense |
| Protocol guides | Expert coach, encouraging |
| Legal pages | Plain language, fair, transparent |
| Error messages | Calm, helpful, never blaming |

**Always**: Use "you" and "your." Active voice. Present tense.
**Never**: Medical jargon without explanation. Fear-based messaging. Guaranteed outcomes.

## Visual Language
- **Colors**: Deep, calming blues + warm neutrals + precise white space. Accent: bioluminescent teal.
- **Typography**: Clean sans-serif. Generous line-height. Never cramped.
- **Imagery**: Real people in real environments. Experience over product shots. Diverse representation.
- **Icons**: Line-based, consistent weight. Never skeuomorphic.

## Language Guardrails
✅ DO USE:
- "designed to support"
- "may help with"
- "research suggests"
- "users report"
- "protocol-guided"
- "access" / "experience" (for rental)

❌ NEVER USE:
- "diagnose", "treat", "cure", "heal disease"
- "proven", "guaranteed", "miracle"
- "FDA approved" (for EU devices)
- "clinical grade" (without regulatory backing)

## USP Hierarchy
1. **Safety** — Protocol-guided, contraindication checking, professional support
2. **Protocol** — Not just a device, but a complete protocol
3. **Access** — Rent before you buy. Try before commit.
4. **Service** — Ongoing support, not just hardware
5. **Integration** — Devices work together as a system

## Competitive Differentiation
- vs. Amazon/eBay: Protocols + support + safety, not just hardware
- vs. Clinical centres: Home access, ongoing relationship, fraction of the cost
- vs. Premium brands: Same quality, accessible pricing through rental model
