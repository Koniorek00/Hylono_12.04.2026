## Hylono Visual Identity

### Aesthetic Direction
- **Premium but human**: Real people, real environments. Not sterile lab stock.
- **Calm confidence**: Spacious layouts, generous whitespace, unhurried feeling.
- **Science made tangible**: Technology shown in human context, not as isolated hardware.
- **Light as metaphor**: Bioluminescent accents. Light = health = the brand.

### Color Palette for Photography
- **Environments**: Warm-neutral interiors (home wellness spaces), soft natural light
- **NOT**: Hospital whites, fluorescent lighting, clinical sterility
- **Color grading**: Slightly desaturated with warm highlights. Premium photography feel.

### Prohibited Visual Patterns
- ❌ Before/after body transformation imagery (implies guaranteed outcomes)
- ❌ Doctors in white coats (implies medical device, not wellness)
- ❌ Generic stock smiling people unrelated to technology
- ❌ Showing devices in obviously fake studio setups only
- ❌ Extreme closeups of medical-looking ports/needles/anything invasive

## Shot Types by Use Case

### Product Hero Shot
- **Angle**: 45° perspective, slightly elevated
- **Background**: Clean, neutral (off-white or deep navy)
- **Lighting**: Soft, directional. Accent light to show premium quality.
- **Scale reference**: Include everyday object or human hand for scale
- **Format**: 2400×1600px minimum, WebP + AVIF

### Lifestyle Shot (in use)
- **Setting**: Home environment — bedroom, living room, home office
- **Person**: Diverse representation (age 25-65, mixed gender, ethnicity)
- **Expression**: Relaxed, calm, not exaggerated happiness
- **Showing**: Device in use, person comfortable and at ease
- **Format**: 2400×1600px landscape, 1200×1500px portrait (mobile)

### Protocol Sequence
- **Format**: Step-by-step numbered sequence (1, 2, 3...)
- **Style**: Clean, diagrammatic. Icon + brief text per step.
- **Purpose**: Show the ritual/protocol, not just the device
- **Usage**: Product pages, onboarding, email templates

### Trust/About Photography
- **Real team members**: Not stock models
- **Setting**: Office or professional home environment
- **Style**: Approachable professional. Not corporate stiff.

## Alt Text Standards
```
Product shots: "[Product name] [key angle/feature] - Hylono"
Example: "Hylono mHBOT Chamber side view showing zipper entry - Hylono"

Lifestyle: "[Activity] with [device] in [setting]"
Example: "Person relaxing in hyperbaric oxygen chamber at home"

Diagrams: "Diagram showing [what it explains]"
Example: "Diagram showing three-step HBOT session protocol"
```
