## Hylono Content Philosophy
We are educators first, marketers second. Our audience is awareness-stage — they don't know what mHBOT is. We must educate before we can sell. Every piece of content should leave readers smarter, not just more interested.

## Awareness Stage Mapping (Schwartz)
| Stage | Reader State | Our Job |
|-------|-------------|---------|
| Unaware | "I feel tired all the time" | Connect symptom to category |
| Problem-aware | "I'm researching recovery tech" | Introduce modalities |
| Solution-aware | "I've heard of HBOT" | Show Hylono's approach |
| Product-aware | "Comparing Hylono to X" | Differentiate, build trust |
| Most aware | "Ready to rent/buy" | Remove friction, reassure |

## Content Templates

### Product Page Structure
```
1. HERO (10-second value test)
   Headline: What transformation does this enable?
   Subhead: How it works in one sentence
   CTA: "Experience It" (rental) | "Learn More"

2. PROMISE
   The problem this solves (2-3 sentences)
   The transformation users experience

3. HOW IT WORKS
   Mechanism in plain language
   "Your [organ/system] responds to [stimulus] by [response]"
   Research reference (not a claim, a curiosity hook)

4. PROTOCOL
   This isn't just a device — it's a protocol
   What a session looks like (duration, frequency, environment)
   What to expect week 1 / week 4 / week 12

5. BENEFITS (experience-led, compliant)
   Not: "Treats inflammation"
   Yes: "Users report waking up feeling more restored"
   Format: Icon + "Users often report..." / "Research suggests..."

6. WHO IT'S FOR
   Primary persona (most specific)
   Secondary personas
   WHO IT'S NOT FOR (contraindications — builds trust)

7. STACK IT
   "Works best with [complementary modality]"
   Show protocol stack if applicable

8. ACCESS OPTIONS
   RENT: [price/week or month] — Try the full protocol
   OWN: [price] — For long-term commitment
   (Rental ALWAYS first, ALWAYS prominent)

9. SPECS (for researchers)
   Technical specifications
   Certifications

10. SAFETY
    Mandatory disclaimer
    Contraindications
    "Consult your healthcare provider"

11. SEO METADATA
    Title tag (50-60 chars)
    Meta description (150-160 chars)
    Schema markup notes
```

## Compliance Checklist (run before publishing)
- [ ] No forbidden words: diagnose, treat, cure, heal disease
- [ ] All specific claims have "research suggests" / "users report" qualifier
- [ ] Study citations added where referenced (or [VERIFY] placeholder)
- [ ] Medical disclaimer present
- [ ] "Consult your healthcare provider" present
- [ ] Contraindications listed
- [ ] No guaranteed outcome language
- [ ] Rental CTA at least as prominent as purchase

## Writing Style Guide

### Headlines
```
❌ "Cure Your Fatigue with mHBOT"
✅ "Breathe Deeper. Recover Faster. The Science of Oxygen Therapy."

❌ "Proven to Reduce Inflammation"
✅ "How Pressurized Oxygen May Support Your Body's Natural Recovery"
```

### Feature → Benefit → Experience
```
Feature: 1.4 ATA pressurization
Benefit: Increases dissolved oxygen in plasma
Experience: Users describe waking up feeling clearer, more restored

Write the experience. Mention the mechanism. Skip the specs-first approach.
```

### Safety Copy Template
```
⚠️ Important Safety Information
[Product] is designed for general wellness support. 
It is not intended to diagnose, treat, cure, or prevent any disease.
Not suitable for: [contraindications list]
Consult your healthcare provider before use if you have a medical condition,
are pregnant, or are taking prescription medications.
```

## Research Reference Standards
- Primary: PubMed peer-reviewed studies
- Format: "A 2023 study published in [Journal] found that..." 
- Always add: "[VERIFY: link needed]" if you haven't confirmed the study
- Never paraphrase a study you haven't read
- Never invent citations
